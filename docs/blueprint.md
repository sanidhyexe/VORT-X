# **App Name**: YUV-X

## Core Features:

- Gamer Profile: User profile creation with gaming-specific fields (preferred games, skill levels, etc.).
- Connect: Ability to connect with other gamers, follow their activity, and build a network.
- Community Hubs: Groups and forums for specific games, genres, or gaming communities.
- Activity Feed: News feed displaying updates, achievements, and activity from connected gamers and groups.
- AI Recommendations: AI tool suggests relevant connections and groups based on the user's gaming profile and activity.
- Enhanced Search: Search functionality to find gamers, groups, and content based on keywords.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Electric Purple (#BE29EC) for a modern and energetic feel, representing the vibrancy of the gaming world.
- Background color: Dark Gray (#222222) for a sleek and immersive experience, reducing eye strain during long gaming sessions.
- Accent color: Cyan (#00FFFF) to highlight interactive elements and calls to action, providing a visual pop against the dark background.
- Body and headline font: 'Space Grotesk', sans-serif, provides a modern and tech-oriented aesthetic, ensuring readability.
- Use clean, vector-based icons with a neon or outlined style to enhance the futuristic and tech-driven theme.
- Employs a grid-based layout to showcase gaming content effectively.
- Subtle animations and transitions, especially on interactions like button presses and content loading, provide a dynamic and engaging user experience.
- Subtle particle animation in the background
